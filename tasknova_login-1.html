1
Minimum Withdrawal: ₹100 se kam withdraw nahi kar sakte.
2

3
Refer & Earn: Dost refer karo, jab wo withdraw kare tab tumhe ₹50 milega.

Tasks: Har task honest tarike se complete karo. Fake activity se account ban ho sakta hai. Available tasks: Watch Ads (20/day), Spin (15/day), Scratch (15/day), Quiz (15/day), Check-In (1/day), Survey.
